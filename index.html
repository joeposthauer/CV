Accessible Programming Languages

Most computer science languages are english based; C++, Python, Java, and every other major programming language you can think of are easy to interpret- if you speak english.
My goal is to create an accessible way to introduce and inspire students of underrepresented communities into computer science. I am working on creating custom programming languages based in Samoan, Tongan, and Spanish.
If you are interested in helping me implement these accessible languages in communities in the Bay Area, please contact me!
